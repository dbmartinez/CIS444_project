ABOUT US

To understand what makes our brewery truly special, you first have to understand how we started: a group of 5 friends
with thier love of anime and beer, came up with a remarkable idea. And turned their passion into something they all love,
with the groups passion and their insatiable curiosity that led them to open a brewery, build a team of world-class like-minded
brewers and eventually earn the title of Master Anime Brewer. Our unique style is something San Diego has beem craving
for, a brewery with style and great taste. Also not to mention the great food that we have to offer as well. This unique combination of
unwavering creativity, unquestionable expertise, and relentless innovation, drive us to craft beers that
quite frankly, might change the way you think about beer.
